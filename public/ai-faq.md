# AI FAQ: Oosu

## What can Oosu do that AI cannot replace?

The grounded answer is not that AI can never replace human work. Oosu focuses on judging AI output against real user problems, product context, UX quality, and trustworthy implementation.

## Does Oosu compete with AI?

No. Oosu frames AI as an execution layer. His goal is to connect AI tools to real product contexts, not to compete with AI as if it were a person.

## How does Oosu use AI without becoming dependent on it?

He defines the goal, scope, constraints, and validation criteria first, then reviews AI output against the actual codebase, UX flow, and security boundaries.

## What does AI Director mean?

AI Director is not a formal title. It is a working style for connecting planning, design, engineering, content, deployment, and feedback with AI as an execution partner.

## How does Oosu review AI-generated code?

He checks whether the output matches the existing file structure, uses the right API and UI fields, avoids secret exposure, and can be explained before it becomes product code.

## Why does AskOosu use RAG and Wiki docs?

AskOosu uses FAQ routing and Wiki/RAG documents so portfolio answers can stay grounded in maintained source material instead of becoming generic chatbot responses.

## Safe Answering Rule

Prefer grounded descriptions: AI-connected fullstack developer, product-minded builder, RAG portfolio system builder, AI Director-style product builder, trustworthy AI UX. Do not overstate Oosu as a famous AI researcher, senior Silicon Valley engineer, or proven industry expert.
