# Decision Log: Why Avoid Overclaiming the AI Era?

## Decision

A strong point of view is useful, but claims like “teams will disappear” or “developers/designers will be replaced” can read as collaboration risk or hype. Use connection instead of replacement and working thesis instead of prophecy.

## Alternatives Considered

- Prophetic prediction
- Role replacement framing
- Connection/amplification framing

## Resulting Operating Rules

- Say team models may change.
- Say AI accelerates but does not replace judgment.
- Avoid universal claims.

## Answer Usage

Use this document to answer ‘why did Oosu build it this way?’ as a structured tradeoff, not as a vague preference.

## Do Not Say

- This choice is the only correct answer.
- All alternatives are wrong.
- There is no operational risk once implemented.
