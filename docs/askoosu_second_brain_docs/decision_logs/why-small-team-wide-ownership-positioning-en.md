# Decision Log: Why Smaller Team, Wider Ownership Positioning?

## Decision

Oosu’s edge is not replacing every role alone. It is owning a wider surface in small projects and bridging PM/design/engineering language in larger teams.

## Alternatives Considered

- Solo replacement framing
- Pure specialist framing
- AI-connected product builder

## Resulting Operating Rules

- Good fit: AI application, product-minded fullstack, RAG/knowledge UX.
- Risk fit: narrow repetitive roles or immediate senior specialist expectations.
- Use conditions rather than absolute claims.

Use this document to answer ‘why did Oosu build it this way?’ as a structured tradeoff, not as a vague preference.
