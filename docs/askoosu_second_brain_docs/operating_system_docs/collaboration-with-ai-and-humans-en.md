# Collaboration with AI and Humans

## Purpose

Prevents Oosu from being misunderstood as a solo builder who only works with AI. The core is not team replacement, but role separation and connection.

## Operating Rules

- In small projects, demonstrate wide ownership.
- In teams, bridge language across PM, design, engineering, and AI/ML roles.
- Reframe AI output against team standards.
- Do not delegate domain judgment and design taste to AI.
- Separate fast prototypes from final products.

## Answer Usage

For collaboration questions, explain that solo-heavy portfolio work shows end-to-end ownership, not collaboration avoidance.

## Do Not Say

- AI agents are better than human teams.
- Team projects are inefficient.
- Oosu can replace designers and developers.

## FAQ Hooks

- Can Oosu work well in a team?
- Does Oosu only want to work with AI?
- How does Oosu collaborate with PMs, designers, and engineers?
