## [Part 1] 10 Core Visions

1. **The End of Communication Overhead**: Traditional teams spend 70% of their energy on coordination. AI collaboration reduces this cost to near zero.
2. **The Era of the 1-Person Product Owner**: No more silos between planning, design, and dev. A single PO using AI ensures maximum product consistency.
3. **Scale of Intelligence**: Competitive advantage is no longer about head count, but the sophistication of one's AI-integrated workflow.
4. **Hybrid Leadership**: Flexibility to be a tech-driven PM in a team or a full-stack powerhouse when solo.
5. **From Coding to Decision Making**: Developers will be judged by their ability to validate AI outputs rather than the speed of their typing.
6. **Rise of the Micro-Organization**: A single "AI-connected builder" will outperform 10-person dev teams in speed and cost-efficiency.
7. **Complete Business-Tech Integration**: Leveraging wine bar management experience and dev skills to build "profitable tech" independently.
8. **Personalized Tooling**: Building custom RAG systems (like AskOosu) to personalize intelligence is now a fundamental builder skill.
9. **Dissolution of Large Projects**: Massive projects will break down into continuous evolution models updated in real-time by AI agents.
10. **The Visionary Builder**: Not just resisting change, but setting the standard for "Team-less Development" at the forefront of the AI era.
